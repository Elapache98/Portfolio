Forbes Explore: Redesigning Content Discovery

All team members and stakeholders
Available on all platforms

When Forbes needed to reimagine how readers discover and engage with content, we created Explore—a personalized content discovery platform that transforms how users navigate our vast editorial ecosystem.

The Challenge

Forbes publishes hundreds of articles daily across diverse topics from business to technology, lifestyle to investing. Our readers were struggling to discover relevant content beyond what appeared on the homepage, leading to decreased engagement and shorter session times.

Key Problems Identified

  • Content discovery relied heavily on manual navigation
  • Personalization was limited to basic preferences
  • Search functionality didn't surface related content effectively
  • Mobile experience lacked intuitive exploration patterns

The Solution

We designed Forbes Explore as an AI-powered content discovery engine that learns from user behavior, reading patterns, and preferences to surface highly relevant articles, authors, and topics.

User research revealed key behavior patterns in content consumption

Early wireframes exploring different discovery mechanisms

Design Process

Research & Discovery

We conducted extensive user interviews with 50+ Forbes readers to understand their content discovery habits. The research revealed that users often felt overwhelmed by choice and wanted more intelligent curation.

Information Architecture

We restructured content categorization to be more intuitive, moving from traditional sections to topic-based clusters that reflect how users actually think about business content.

Personalization Algorithm

The recommendation engine considers multiple factors:

  • Reading history and time spent on articles
  • Topic preferences and author follows
  • Trending content within user's industry
  • Social signals and peer recommendations

Key Features

Smart Recommendations

The platform uses machine learning to understand user preferences and surface content that matches their interests, reading level, and professional focus.

Topic Clustering

Related articles are automatically grouped into coherent topic clusters, making it easier for users to dive deep into subjects they care about.

Progressive Disclosure

Information is revealed progressively as users engage, preventing cognitive overload while maintaining discoverability.

Lessons Learned

This project reinforced the importance of user-centered design in content platforms. By focusing on how people naturally discover and consume information, we created a more intuitive and engaging experience that serves both readers and the business.

The success of Forbes Explore has influenced our approach to other product initiatives, emphasizing personalization and intelligent content curation across the Forbes ecosystem.
